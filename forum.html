Collaborative Forum Summaries

This page includes summaries of discussions and insights gained from peer collaborative forums in the Deciphering Big Data module.

Overview

Throughout the module, I actively participated in the collaborative forum with my peers, namely on the data collection process. The discussion highlighted the rapid growth of IoT, projected to reach 60 billion devices by 2030, and its opportunities across business, healthcare, agriculture, and smart cities. Key challenges include managing large, diverse datasets and ensuring data privacy and security. Solutions such as fog computing combined with machine learning can improve efficiency and scalability, but robust governance and ethical practices are essential for safe and effective IoT implementation.
